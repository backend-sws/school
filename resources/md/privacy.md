# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 12, 2026  
**Last Updated:** March 12, 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

PDS Education Technologies Pvt. Ltd. ("we", "us", "our") operates the PDS Education Institutional Operating System ("Platform"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Platform.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information

When you register or use the Platform, we may collect:

- **Identity Data:** Full name, date of birth, gender, photograph.
- **Contact Data:** Email address, phone number, postal address.
- **Institutional Data:** Institution name, role, department, designation.
- **Financial Data:** Card details (encrypted), billing address, transaction history.
- **Authentication Data:** Username, password (hashed), session tokens.

### 2.2 Student & Staff Records

Institutions may upload:

- Student enrollment data, academic records, attendance logs.
- Staff employment details, qualifications, and assignments.
- Fee payment records, scholarship information.
- Health records, emergency contacts (as provided by the institution).

### 2.3 Automatically Collected Data

- **Device Information:** Browser type, operating system, device identifiers.
- **Usage Data:** Pages visited, features used, time spent, click patterns.
- **Log Data:** IP addresses, access timestamps, error logs.
- **Cookies:** Session cookies, preference cookies, analytics cookies.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

- **Provide Services:** Operate and maintain the Platform's core features.
- **Personalize Experience:** Customize dashboards, notifications, and reports.
- **Process Payments:** Handle subscriptions, invoicing, and billing.
- **Communicate:** Send service updates, security alerts, and support responses.
- **Improve Platform:** Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and performance.
- **Comply with Law:** Meet legal obligations, respond to lawful requests.

## 4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- **Encryption:** All sensitive data (card details, passwords) is encrypted using AES-256.
- **Transport Security:** All data in transit is protected via TLS 1.3.
- **Access Controls:** Role-based access ensures users only see data relevant to their permissions.
- **Audit Logs:** All data access and modifications are logged for accountability.
- **Regular Audits:** We conduct periodic security assessments and penetration testing.

## 5. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do **not** sell your personal information. We may share data with:

- **Service Providers:** Hosting, payment processing, email delivery (under strict data processing agreements).
- **Institution Administrators:** User data within their institution's scope.
- **Legal Requirements:** When required by law, court order, or governmental authority.
- **Business Transfers:** In connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale (with prior notice).

## 6. Data Retention

- **Active Accounts:** Data is retained as long as your account is active.
- **Deleted Accounts:** Personal data is deleted within 30 days of account termination.
- **Legal Hold:** Certain data may be retained longer if required by law.
- **Backups:** Backup copies are purged within 90 days of deletion.

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data.
- **Correction:** Update inaccurate or incomplete information.
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
- **Export:** Download your data in a machine-readable format.
- **Restrict Processing:** Limit how we process your data in certain circumstances.
- **Withdraw Consent:** Revoke consent for optional data processing at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@pdseducation.com.

## 8. Children's Privacy

- We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without parental/guardian consent.
- Institutions are responsible for obtaining necessary consents for student data.
- Parents/guardians may request access to or deletion of their child's data.

## 9. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies for:

| Cookie Type | Purpose | Duration |
|---|---|---|
| Essential | Authentication, security, session management | Session |
| Functional | User preferences, language settings | 1 year |
| Analytics | Usage statistics, feature adoption tracking | 1 year |

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

## 10. International Data Transfers

- Data is primarily stored on servers located in India.
- If data is transferred internationally, we ensure adequate protection through standard contractual clauses or equivalent safeguards.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

- We may update this Privacy Policy periodically.
- Material changes will be notified via email or in-app notification at least 15 days in advance.
- Continued use of the Platform constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
